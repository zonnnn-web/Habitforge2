TIME-BLOCKING WISDOM
HOW TO TIME-BLOCK
  • 01Plan tomorrow the night before — 10 minutes max.
  • 02Identify your ONE most important task. Do it first.
  • 03Group similar tasks — batch emails, batch flashcards.
  • 04Block time for breaks — real rest, not half-rest.
  • 05Protect the first 2 hours for deep, cognitively hard work.
  • 06Every evening: review what you did vs. what you planned.
  • 07Interleave subjects (Make It Stick) — don't block 4h on one topic.
